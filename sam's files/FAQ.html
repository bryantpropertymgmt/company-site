Frequently Asked Questions

If your questions are not addressed below please do not hesitate to contact us.

(Q) What forms of payment are accepted?

(A) At this time check, cashier's check or money order are the forms of payment we accept.

(Q) What is the late fee charge if I am late on my rent payment?

(A) The current late fee is $40.00 in addition to your rent rate. For each additional month that passes you will incur an additional $40.00 late charge.

(Q) What are your office hours?

(A) Our phone lines are available from Monday thru Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. PROVIDE PHONE NUMBER. You can also reach us by email. PROVIDE EMAIL

(Q) What is the process to report the need of a repair?

(A) When it is noticed that something in broken such as (but not limited to) a washer, dryer or dishwasher please call our office to report the problem. We will make arrangements to visit and assess the issue. Then BPM will take the appropriate action to correct the problem. Please allow 24 hours for a response in non-emergency situation. If it is an emergency please contact 911 or other proper authorities.

(Q) Are their public amenities?

(A) It depends on the property. Please contact us for information of the specific property you are interested in renting. PROVIDE PHONE NUMBER or EMAIL

(Q) Does BPM cover any of the utilities?

(A) It depends on the property. Please contact us for information of the specific property you are interested in renting. PROVIDE PHONE NUMBER or EMAIL

(Q) How much is the security deposit?

(A) The security deposit will be equal to your first months rent and is due prior to moving into the property.

(Q) When is my rent due?

(A) This will be stated in the lease however, the typical due date for rent payments in the 5th of every month.

(Q) Is a background check required?

(A) Yes, we run a background check for every potential tenant through TransUnion Smart Move. The cost of the background check $70 and is expected to be paid when the application of lease is completed and returned to our representatives.

(Q) Do you check my credit report?

(A) This is information is included in the background check we complete through a third party - TransUnion Smart Move. See the previous question above.

(Q) Do you support equal housing opportunities?

(A) Yes, Bryant Property Management, LLC. is an equal housing opportunity company. We consider all applicants. Whether you are looking for a fresh start or just looking to move to a new area, we hope we can help you find a place you can call home
